Fried Egg with Minced Pork

Description Fried Egg with Minced Pork

Ingrediens

  • Egg 2
  • pork minced 20 g
  • soy source
  • pepper

Steps

  1. stir fried pork in low heat
  2. put soy source and cook until pork is cooked
  3. remove pork from pan
  4. add egg to pan and wait for white egg to cooked
  5. pour cooked pork to egg
